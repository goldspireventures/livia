# Journey — P1 Founder × Multi-shop small chain × Hair

**The cell.** Aoife Kelly. Three Aurora salons (Dublin centre, Dublin south, Galway). 60 staff total. Currently three Phorest tenants + Excel rollup. Sunday-evening triage takes 90 min.

## First touch

- Recommendation from a Founder peer at a chain-owners dinner.
- Visits `livia.io/for/founders`. Sees the cross-shop dashboard preview + voice-receptionist demo. Stays 11 minutes.
- Books a 60-min founder-led demo.

## Sign-up & onboarding (concierge — 6 weeks)

- Three-shop migration is concierge-only. F9 design-partner programme: she's offered first-3-Founders status (steep discount, equity grant of 0.05%, weekly 30-min interview during year 1).
- Phorest data export broker pulls customer + booking history per shop (~6,000 customers + 38,000 historical bookings). 14-day reconciliation window.
- Per-shop voice numbers provisioned + ported.
- Per-shop WhatsApp Business sender verified.
- Per-shop staff onboarding (60 staff): Liv-character-explanation video (5 min) + 1:1 onboarding call for each Manager, group call for Senior STAFF, opt-in for Junior STAFF.
- Per-shop refund cap ladder set: REC €0; ADM (each Manager) €100; OWN ∞.
- Per-shop brand customisation: Aurora's logo + slight Aurora-Midnight overlay.
- Phorest stays parallel for 30 days (data sync; bookings in both); then cutover.

## First day post-cutover

- All three shops on Liv. Voice live for all three.
- Aoife's cross-shop cockpit live: 3 numbers per shop, 1 line per shop.
- First Sunday digest (delivered night-1): chain-rollup + per-shop sub-sections.

## First week

- 280 voice calls across 3 shops; ~210 bookings.
- 3 Manager-side training touchpoints: first refund-ladder use; first time-off-request flow; first cross-shop pattern surface ("Galway's no-show rate ran 18% this week — well above 10% baseline").
- First Sunday digest is a moment: Aoife reads it 3 times. Texts the founder: "it knew. I didn't."

## First month

- Cross-shop staff borrow happens once (Senior at Dublin centre helps cover at Dublin south for one Saturday) — Liv mediates the borrow flow (H03 cross-shop staff borrow). Aoife approves. The Senior gets a thank-you note in her own digest.
- First Owner-on-holiday test: 4 days. Manager Niamh (Dublin centre) gets the elevated authority for the chain-rollup digest during the window. Cap-escalations route to Niamh. No incidents above her elevated cap.
- Aoife's Sunday triage drops from 90 min to ~25 min by week 4.

## First quarter

- The promotion to R3 happens shop-by-shop. Dublin centre first (Niamh has built trust with Liv over 60 days). Dublin south month 4. Galway slower — the new colourist needed time, then quit, then was replaced.
- First "Liv was wrong" with chain-implication: she suggested a marketing message to Galway regulars during the colour-team gap that came across more sales-y than Aoife's brand. Aoife flags; Liv learns; voice register tuned brand-tighter.
- First cross-shop financial rollup auto-generated (vs Aoife's old Excel) — saves her ~6 hrs/quarter.

## First year

- Ratchet to R4 at month 9: Liv proposes plans on the chain-rollup level (e.g. "Dublin south's Junior conversion to second-visit is 12% lower than the other two — propose a Junior-mentoring sprint for Q3"). Aoife approves the first plan; iterates on the second.
- Year-end: Aoife considers opening shop #4. Configuration graduation: chain-rollup planning + new-shop onboarding flow (see `configuration-graduation.md`).
- Renewal: she signs a 2-year deal (annual discount applies; F9 packaging).
- Casts the case-study video (per design-partner programme exit terms).

**Rung at year end:** R4 active for chain-level decisions; R3 baseline at every shop.

## Lifecycle moments designed for in this journey

- **First Sunday digest (night 1).** The single most-cited "trust earned" moment for Founder cell.
- **First Owner-on-holiday (month 1, short).** Test of the elevated-cap mechanism.
- **First cross-shop staff borrow (month 1-2).** The chain-flexibility moment.
- **First "Liv was wrong" with brand-implication (month 3-4).** Brand-protection refinement.
- **First Christmas push (month ~6 — depending on signup).** Calendar overlay critical for chain.
- **First shop expansion proposal (month 12).** Configuration graduation.
