# Journey — P2a Owner-with-Mgr × Single-shop with mgr × Hair

**The cell.** Roisín Doherty. Aurora Studio (single shop). 14 staff. Niamh as Manager. Currently Phorest + WhatsApp + Excel for the gaps.

## First touch

- Niamh hears about Liv from a Receptionist friend at another salon. Niamh shows Roisín on her lunch break.
- Roisín visits the marketing site, watches the demo (focuses on the cap-ladder + weekly digest sections).
- Books a 30-min sales call.

## Sign-up & onboarding (hybrid — 2 weeks)

- Phorest data export broker. Customer + booking history pulled.
- Voice number ported.
- WhatsApp verified.
- Refund-cap ladder configured with Roisín + Niamh together (Niamh €100, Roisín ∞). Roisín explicitly chooses to enable Senior-w-admin role: promotes Sarah (her colour lead) to ADM-D scoped to colour-team (cap €50).
- Staff onboarding: Niamh runs it (founder available); 14 staff onboarded in 3 hour-long group calls + individual touchpoints.
- Phorest stays parallel for 30 days; cutover.

## First day post-cutover

- Niamh's first day with Liv as floor-manager surface. Reads the morning briefing on her phone over coffee at home.
- First voice call answered hour 1.
- Roisín's first day-end summary delivered to her phone at 7pm.

## First week

- 320 customer touches (calls + DMs) handled. 245 bookings.
- First scoped time-off request via Sarah (ADM-D): one of her colour team requests Wed-off; Sarah approves (her authority covers Wed-only); Niamh ratifies. Liv takes care of the rebook drafts.
- First above-cap refund: €280 — escalates to Roisín; she taps approve in 4 minutes.

## First month

- Niamh promoted to R3 by week 3 (Liv handles routine refunds within Niamh's cap autonomously now; Niamh sees the audit log weekly).
- First Sunday digest read by Roisín — she texts Niamh: "we should talk Thursday about Saturday capacity."
- Sarah (ADM-D) reaches R2 — handles colour-team time-off and refunds within her scope; Niamh's load decreases meaningfully.

## First quarter

- Roisín reaches R4: Liv proposes plans monthly. First plan: Saturday late-afternoon over-capacity (4 weeks running) — Liv proposes extending one Senior shift Saturday or hiring a second Saturday Junior. Roisín opts for the shift extension.
- First "Liv was wrong" moment: she booked a regular with Sarah's team when the regular asked for "anyone but Sarah" (Sarah had a previous awkward conversation with the customer). Sarah flags; Liv apologises, rebooks with another Senior, learns the preference, surfaces it (consent-gated) to other colour-team members.

## First year

- Niamh continues at R3. Sarah at R3 (within scope).
- Roisín at R4 stable.
- First Owner-on-holiday: 10 days; Niamh gets elevated cap to €500. No incidents above.
- Renewal at year-end. Roisín considers a second shop (configuration graduation to C7 small chain).

## Lifecycle moments

- **First scoped time-off via ADM-D (week 1).** Validates the senior-w-admin role.
- **First above-cap refund (week 1).** Validates the cap-ladder UX.
- **First Sunday digest (night 1).** Trust amplification.
- **First plan proposal (month 3).** R4 emergence.
- **First "Liv was wrong" with staff-relationship implication (month 2).** Refinement of customer-staff preference learning.
