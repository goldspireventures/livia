## What partners get

- **12 months at 50% off** Year-1 list price.
- **Free concierge migration** (Phorest, Fresha, Booksy, Square — whichever applies; greenfield free too).
- **Direct line to founders.** WhatsApp + monthly 1:1.
- **Influence on roadmap.** Their priorities go to the top of the queue subject to "would this help all customers" filter.
- **First-100 badge** in the product (visible in their cockpit; they can choose to display in-shop signage).
- **Possible small equity grant** for first 3 (founder discretion; ≤0.05% per partner; vest over 4 years).
- **Co-branded launch announcement** at month 6 (with their permission).

## What we extract

- **Weekly 30-min interview** for the first 12 weeks; bi-weekly thereafter.
- **Permission to record screens** during normal use (with PII redaction; non-negotiable).
- **Quarterly NPS** (the only formal survey; light-touch).
- **Case-study rights** at month 6, 12, 24 (with their approval per case study).
- **Video testimonials** at month 12 (optional; their choice).
- **Referrals** with the standard referral incentive (1 month free for both parties per successful referral).
- **A real audit-log review session** at month 6 — they walk us through what they've seen Liv do; what surprised them; what felt off.

## Selection process

1. Founder-led outreach (warm introductions only — no cold).
2. 30-minute mutual-fit conversation (us assessing their fit; them assessing ours).
3. Reference check (we contact two of their suppliers / colleagues).
4. Offer letter + 7-day decision window.
5. Onboarding kicks off within 14 days of acceptance.

## The exit

Partner graduates to "regular customer" at month 12. What changes:

- **Pricing** moves to standard list (with grandfather option: stay at the discounted rate for 3 more years if they renew annual prepay).
- **Direct line** narrows to monthly check-in (still real, less frequent).
- **Roadmap influence** shifts from "top of queue" to "customer council" (a formal monthly forum for top-50 customers).
- **First-100 badge** stays forever.

## Failure mode — what if a partner is unhappy?

- We honour the 50%-off-12-month commitment regardless.
- We help them migrate to whichever competitor they choose, free of charge.
- We schedule an exit interview; the failure is documented as an RFC in `docs/postmortems/`.
- The exit terms are explicit in the partner letter; no surprises.

## What this earns us

A grounded, evidence-based v1 product. Honest, non-extractive testimonials. A network effect (each partner refers 1–2 more). A roadmap that serves real cells, not invented ones. The trust-amplification brand starts with these 10.
