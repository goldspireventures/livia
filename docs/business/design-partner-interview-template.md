# Design partner interview template

**Cadence:** Weekly 30 min, weeks 1–12 (then bi-weekly)  
**Storage:** `.local/research/design-partners/{slug}-{YYYY-MM-DD}.md` (not in public repo)  
**Success metric:** Track **Liv-by-name** vs “the AI” / “the system” (Bet 1, 60% by month 6)

---

## Before the call

- [ ] Review last week’s notes and open **support tickets** for this tenant (internal portal).
- [ ] Pull: bookings via Liv this week, voice recoveries, any `liv_error` tickets.

---

## Script (30 min)

### 1. Warm-up (3 min)

- How was the week? Busier or quieter than usual?

### 2. Tuesday morning (5 min)

- Did you open Livia on mobile/web in the morning?
- What did you look at first? Was anything wrong or missing?
- *Probe:* “Did Liv tell you anything useful before you opened the door?”

### 3. Liv by name (5 min) — **critical**

- *Unprompted:* “Who handled that booking / message?”
- Note exact words: Liv / AI / bot / system / app.
- If they say “Liv”: “What would you ask her to do next month that she doesn’t do today?”

### 4. Voice / channels (5 min)

- Any calls or texts Liv handled? Anything go wrong?
- Would a customer know they spoke to AI? Was that OK?

### 5. Support & trust (5 min)

- Did you use **Report issue**? Was response fast enough?
- Have you read the **audit log**? Would you show it to your accountant?

### 6. Incumbent comparison (4 min)

- What did Phorest/Fresha/paper do that you still miss?
- What would make you recommend Livia to another owner?

### 7. Close (3 min)

- One thing we should fix next week?
- Permission to use anonymised quote? Y/N

---

## After the call

- [ ] Log in partner tracker: Liv-by-name Y/N, blocking issues, NPS gut (1–10).
- [ ] Create support ticket if they reported a bug not yet filed.
- [ ] If blocking: confirm Support SLA clock started.
