# Design partner — support SLA (paste into offer letter)

**For founder:** paste into design-partner agreement or welcome email.

---

**Support during the programme**

While you are an active design partner, Livia Inc provides business-hours support (Monday–Friday, 09:00–17:00 Ireland time, excluding public holidays):

- **Blocking issues** (you cannot run bookings or Liv is down for customers): we aim to respond within **four (4) business hours**.
- **Non-blocking issues**: we aim to respond within **two (2) business days**.

Please use **Report issue** in the Livia app (Settings or sidebar) so we receive your route and context automatically. You may also email **support@livia.io** and include your ticket reference if you have one.

Outside business hours, Liv continues on automated channels where configured; human support resumes on the next business day.

This SLA is a programme commitment for design partners and does not create a general service-level agreement for all future commercial terms.
