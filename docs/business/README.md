## What we provide vs what you bring

| Livia provides (ready to go) | You bring (your business) |
|------------------------------|---------------------------|
| Booking policy defaults per country (IE, GB, DE, …) | Your final cancellation/deposit wording |
| Liv tone packs per vertical | Your brand voice overrides in Settings → Liv |
| Staff invite + roles (Clerk) | Employment contracts, local labour law |
| Leave request → approve → calendar block | Your internal HR process |
| Running late SMS (when Twilio is live) | Accurate start times on the floor |
| Public booking + AI disclosure text | Your privacy policy link |
| Starter templates below | Signed policies for your jurisdiction |

Templates are **starting points** — have a solicitor review anything that binds you with customers or staff.
