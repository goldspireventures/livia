## What we say in conversation (design partners)

**Closed beta:** 12 months at **50% off** list (see design-partner programme). No charge until billing goes live unless you agree otherwise in writing.

**After beta:** You pay for **Liv to live at your business**, **seats for staff who work with her**, and optionally a **small share when she recovers bookings you would have missed on the phone** (voice only).

## Simplified tiers (maps to F9 + `PLAN_CATALOGUE`)

| Tier | Who | From (list, monthly) | Includes |
|------|-----|----------------------|----------|
| **Solo** | 1–2 chairs, owner-led | **€79** | Liv, cockpit, mobile, audit |
| **Studio** | Team + manager | **€149** | + dedicated voice number, WhatsApp path, drift tools |
| **Chain** | Per shop | **€249** / shop | + rollup, chain briefing |
| **Host** | Chair-rental | **€99** + **€19** / renter | + rent automation, per-renter scope |

**Seats (monthly):** Manager €15 · Senior-w-admin €12 · Staff €8 · Receptionist €10 · Apprentice €4.  
*Stripe v1 bills flat €15/seat on team tiers until role-based metering ships (v1.5).*

**Voice recovery (optional):** **4%** of recovered booking value — **monthly cap** per plan, visible in your weekly digest (e.g. Solo €50/mo, Studio €150/mo).
