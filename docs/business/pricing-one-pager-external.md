## What we say in conversation (design partners)

**Closed beta:** 12 months at **50% off** list (see design-partner programme). No charge until billing goes live unless you agree otherwise in writing.

**After beta:** You pay for **Liv to live at your business**, **seats for staff who work with her**, and optionally a **small share when she recovers bookings you would have missed on the phone** (voice only).

**Voice recovery (optional component):** **4%** of recovered booking value, cap **€5** per booking — only when measurement is shown in your weekly digest.
