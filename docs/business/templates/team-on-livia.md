# Bringing your team onto Livia

**Use this internally** — adapt for your staff handbook or onboarding email.

---

## We do not hire through Livia

Livia is **not** a job board. When you join our team, the owner sends an **invite email** from **Team → Invite**. You create your login and land on **My chair** (or the floor view if you are reception).

---

## Roles

| Role | Typical person | Can do |
|------|----------------|--------|
| Owner | Proprietor | Everything including billing and ownership transfer |
| Admin / Manager | Salon manager, practice manager | Approve leave, run inbox, edit policy |
| Staff | Stylist, clinician, therapist | Own appointments, **request own leave** |
| Reception | Front desk | Calendar, inbox handoff |

---

## Your first day

1. Accept the invite and sign in.  
2. Open **My chair** — see your next client.  
3. Check **Profile → Leave** if you need time off (do not ask manager to type it for you).  
4. If running late, use **Running late** on Today or the appointment (manager may message all of today's clients).

---

## Privacy

Customer messages may be handled by **Liv** (AI) until a human takes over. EU businesses show an AI disclosure on chat and booking.
