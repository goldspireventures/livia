# Post-visit feedback (starter — workflow coming)

**Status:** Product workflow Phase C. Use manually until Liv automates.

---

## Goal

Ask every completed client once: *How was your visit?* Route negatives to the owner quickly; positives to Google/reviews **only when you choose**.

---

## Manual process today

1. Same evening: SMS or email — *"Thanks for visiting [Business]. How did we do? Reply 1–5."*  
2. Score ≤3 → manager calls within 24h.  
3. Score ≥4 → optional review link (once per quarter per client to avoid fatigue).

---

## When Liv ships feedback automation

- Trigger: booking marked **Completed** + [24h] delay.  
- Liv message in your tone; owner sees summary on **Today**.  
- Opt-out honoured per SMS marketing rules in your country pack.
