# Running late — floor procedure

---

## When to use

- You are behind for **one** upcoming client → notify **that appointment**.  
- The whole day is slipping → notify **all confirmed clients today** (use with care).

---

## In Livia (web or mobile)

1. **Today** or **Bookings** → **Running late**.  
2. Or open the **booking** → **Running late** → *This appointment*.  
3. Enter minutes late → send.  
4. SMS sends only if your business has SMS configured; otherwise check inbox manually.

---

## Tips

- Send before the client arrives when possible.  
- Do not use broadcast for one late stylist unless the whole shop is delayed.  
- Reception owns bulk messages on busy Saturdays.
