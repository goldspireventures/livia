# Leave and rota — how we use Livia

---

## Principles

1. **You request your own leave** (app → your profile → Leave, or My chair).  
2. **Managers approve** on **Rota → Leave approvals** — they do not submit leave on your behalf unless break-glass (document separately).  
3. When approved, Livia **blocks the calendar** automatically.  
4. Liv may **propose** leave or coverage later — a human always approves.

---

## Staff

- Submit dates, type (annual, sick, training, …), optional note.  
- Check status: pending / approved.  
- Do not book clients over approved leave — the system should prevent it.

---

## Managers

- Review pending requests daily.  
- Approve or follow up in person before rejecting.  
- Publish **shifts** on Rota when you use shift planning (optional; availability rules still apply at booking).

---

## Owner

- Set policy in **Settings → Policy** (notice periods, caps — when available).  
- Payroll export from **Liv command** if you use external payroll.
