## Cadence overlays

- **Quarterly foundation audit.** Last week of each quarter. Re-read all foundation docs; retire stale claims; update this calendar.
- **Monthly roadmap review.** First Monday. Review next 60 days; adjust within current version.
- **Weekly eng demo + design-partner cadence.** Tactical; doesn't move the calendar.

## What's NOT scheduled

- Major brand refresh — not until ≥3000 tenants OR a category competitor forces it.
- Apple Watch app — post-v3.
- Public API GA — v3.
- US market — never.

## Cancellation conditions

We cancel a planned version if:
- Acceptance criteria from the prior version don't hold for 90 days.
- A foundation audit reveals a strategic-shift requirement (e.g., new category competitor invalidates a wedge bet).
- Hiring plan slippage means we can't staff the version commitments at quality.

Cancellations are RFC'd, then announced to the team, then to design partners + customers in the following month's roadmap update.
