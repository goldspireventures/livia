# Persona × Vertical × Configuration matrix — the populated cell list

**Status:** F1 (2026-05-07). The single most-cited F1 artifact.

A theoretical 11 verticals × 13 configurations × 8 personas = 1144 cells. In practice only ~70 are populated and meaningful. This file enumerates them with priority tags and one-line descriptions.

**Priority legend:**
- **v1-critical** — must serve at launch; product fails without it.
- **v1-supported** — must serve at launch; failure is recoverable.
- **v1.5** — deferred per F7 reflection (chair-rental, partnership configs land 3-6mo post-launch).
- **v2** — 6-12 mo post-launch.
- **v3** — 12-24 mo post-launch.
- **never** — explicit no.
- **N/A** — sparse cell; persona doesn't exist in this configuration.

**Evidence flag:**
- `[INFERRED]` — drawn from web research and prior knowledge; needs design-partner validation.
- `[VALIDATED]` — confirmed by at least one design-partner conversation. (None at F1.)

## P7 Customer × ... (universal cross-cell)

The Customer is universal across all configurations and verticals. Two cuts of customer treatment ship at v1:
- **App-aware customer** — interacts via WhatsApp, SMS, voice line, or public booking page; sees Liv's character.
- **No-app customer** — interacts only via SMS or voice; never visits a webpage or app.

Both v1-critical. See `customer-typology-skeleton.md` for archetypes (new, regular, VIP, refund-prone, multi-salon, drifted).
